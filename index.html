Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Mouda does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your financial data stays entirely on your device.

Data Storage

Mouda stores all data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your financial information, including asset names, categories, and monthly records, never leaves your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial data or transaction details
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data

Third-Party Services

Mouda does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline and does not make any network requests.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all data at any time by deleting the app. No data remains on any external server.

Children's Privacy

Mouda does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
